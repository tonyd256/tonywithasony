New Documentary

Dipsea Generations

The journey of five young runners from across the Bay Area as they take on the iconic Dipsea Race.

The Bay Area is home to a diverse population and some of the best trails in the US, yet trail running here is still disappointingly homogenous. The Dipsea Race is the oldest, and perhaps the most unique, trail race in the US, and its unique handicap start makes it ripe for improving access to the sport.

Dipsea Generations celebrates the rich history of this race while using it as a platform to uplift the people and organizations working to improve longstanding inequities in trail running. Through the stories of these community leaders, programs, and families, we explore how running can be a vehicle for confidence, community, and a deeper relationship to nature and how these communities flourish when youth of all backgrounds have access.
